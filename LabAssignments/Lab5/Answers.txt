Q1: Counting spaces and stars
Example: h = 7, t = 6, ch = '*'
Line 1: gap1 = 0, dash1 = 6, gap2 = 12, dash2 = 6
Line 2: gap1 = 1, dash1 = 6, gap2 = 10, dash2 = 6
Line 3: gap1 = 2, dash1 = 6, gap2 = 8,  dash2 = 6
...
Line 7: 2*t = 12 stars
(Repeat process for h = 9, t = 7)

Q2: Patterns
- gap1 increases by 1 each row.
- dash1 and dash2 remain equal to thickness t.
- gap2 decreases by 2 each row.
- The final line has 2*t characters (bottom of Vee).

Q3: Pseudocode
read h, t, ch
for i = 0 to h-2
    gap1 = i
    dash1 = t
    gap2 = 2*(h-1-i)
    dash2 = t
    printChars(gap1, ' ')
    printChars(dash1, ch)
    printChars(gap2, ' ')
    printChars(dash2, ch)
    newline
end for
printChars(2*t, ch)   // bottom line
newline
